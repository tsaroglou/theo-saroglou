About Me

Postdoctoral researcher at the Interdisciplinary University of Austria working within the Game Theory and Evolutionary Dynamics research group. Current work focuses on designing and conducting innovative economic experiments that explore strategic interactions and evolutionary dynamics in human behavior. With a strong background in experimental design and analysis, research investigates how decision-making processes and beliefs shape outcomes in social and economic contexts.
