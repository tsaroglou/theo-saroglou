About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Barcelona with a focus on belief-based utility.

Belief-based utility emphasizes that utility is shaped not only by material outcomes but also by individuals' expectations and beliefs.

Among others, my interests include inequality beliefs, self-image, self-deception, altruism, and social-image in the workplace.

I am experienced in designing, coding and conducting novel economic experiments.

Currently seeking academic and research positions.
