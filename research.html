Research Overview

As a PhD candidate at the University of Barcelona, my research focuses on topics related to behavioral and experimental economics, with specific attention to self-deception, altruism, and inequality beliefs.

Key Research Areas

  • Self-deception and Altruism: Investigating how individuals' beliefs about themselves influence their altruistic behavior.
  • Inequality Beliefs: Exploring how perceptions of inequality impact decision-making and social preferences.
  • Social-image and Task Uptake: Studying how social-image concerns influence individuals' willingness to engage in tasks.
